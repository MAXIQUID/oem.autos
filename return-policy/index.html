OEM.autos Parts Returns Policy

At OEM.autos, we strive to ensure our customers receive high-quality automotive parts that meet their needs. Please review our returns policy below before initiating a return.

1. Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return, parts must meet the following conditions:

  • The part must be unused, uninstalled, and in its original packaging.
  • The return request must be made within 30 days of the delivery date.
  • Proof of purchase (invoice or order confirmation) must be provided.

2. Non-Returnable Items

Some items cannot be returned for safety or hygiene reasons, including:

  • Electrical components once installed
  • Lubricants, fluids, or chemicals
  • Custom or special-order parts
  • Any part showing signs of damage or installation

3. How to Initiate a Return

To start a return, please follow these steps:

  • Contact our Customer Support at support@oem.autos with your order number and reason for return.
  • Receive a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number from our team.
  • Securely package the part and include the RMA number inside the box.
  • Ship the package to the address provided by Customer Support.

4. Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. Approved refunds will be processed within 7 business days using the original payment method.

5. Exchanges

If you need to exchange a part for a different item, please follow the same process above and indicate the replacement item in your request.

Note: OEM.autos is not responsible for shipping costs on returned items unless the return is due to a defective or incorrect part.

6. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a part that is defective or damaged during shipping:

  • Contact Customer Support immediately with photos of the damage.
  • We will arrange a replacement or refund at no additional cost to you.
